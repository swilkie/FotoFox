How fotofox works

Show us your style

We want to know you on a personal level. This is our chance to understand your needs, personality, and tastes. Let’s start by getting the generic questions out of the way at the link below, then we will personally follow up with you to help and to learn how we can make your ideas become real.

We’ll find your best options

Once we understand your vision and personality, we use our experience and expertise to search through the thousands of photographers and narrow the options down to the top 3 to 5 photographers that we think will make you happiest.

Own your wedding

We know that you’re the only who can plan your perfect wedding. With the thousands of photographers cut down to a manageable size, you can now review each photographer’s portfolios, pricing, and personality in depth, to communicate with the photographer and make sure that they can make your perfect day just that.

Make it happen

Once you’ve found the photographer of your dreams, we make it a reality. We take care of scheduling, review all contracts before a photographer asks you to sign anything, verify photographers have valid insurance, and enable safe payments. From beginning to end, we are available at all times as your guide, explaining what you can expect and answer all questions you may have.
